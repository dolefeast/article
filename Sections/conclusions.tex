\section{Discussion and outlook}

We have studied the semiclassical Klein-Gordon-Maxwell system equations through an iterative procedure to study the effect of backreaction to a background classical electric field with a well-defined prescription of vacuum polarization. This precise set up was already studied in~\cite{ambjorn_properties_1983}, where vacuum polarization is calculated using a mode sum formula, expression that cannot be derived from gauge invariant renormalization procedures.
